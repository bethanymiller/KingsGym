Competitive Boxing

The Kings Boxing Club Competitors Class is for those students who compete in USA BOXING amateur boxing competitions. This class is for students with an advanced skill set looking to take it to the next level.

Sparing is a component of the Competitors class and is done in a safe and constructive manner.

To be a part of the Kings Boxing Club Competitors Class you must be cleared to do so by our Head Trainer Robert Francis.
